## üíµ FINANCE HUB BREAKDOWN

### 1. COGS MANAGER

Track complete product cost:

**Materials:**
- Fabric cost per yard
- Yards per garment
- Trims (buttons, zippers, labels)
- Packaging (bags, tags, boxes)

**Manufacturing:**
- Labor per unit
- Pattern making (amortized)
- Grading (sizes)
- Samples (amortized)
- Printing/embroidery
- Quality control

**Freight & Logistics:**
- International freight
- Import duties/tariffs
- Customs brokerage
- Domestic shipping

**Inventory:**
- Warehousing fees
- Handling labor
- Shrinkage rate

**System Calculates:**
```
True Cost Per Item (TCPI) = all costs
Gross Margin = price - TCPI  
Margin % = (margin / price) * 100
```

---

### 2. AI PRICING ENGINE

**How It Works:**

1. **Collect Data:**
   - Your COGS
   - Current price
   - Sales velocity
   - Competitor prices
   - Historical data

2. **AI Analysis (DeepSeek):**
   - Calculates optimal price
   - Predicts demand change
   - Estimates revenue impact
   - Provides reasoning

3. **Recommendations:**
   - "Increase Heritage Hoodie from $145 to $152"
   - "Expected: 21 units/day, 66% margin"
   - "Reasoning: Competitor avg $158, you have room"

4. **Scenarios:**
   - Aggressive pricing
   - Conservative pricing
   - Volume play

---

### 3. EXPENSE TRACKING

**Categories:**

- Marketing (ads, influencers, photoshoots)
- Platform & Tools (Stripe, SendGrid, hosting)
- Team & Overhead (salaries, rent, utilities)
- Returns & CS (shipping, repackaging)
- One-Time (trade shows, redesigns)

**Dashboard Shows:**
- Total monthly expenses
- Expenses by category
- Expense trends
- Budget vs actual
- Expense as % of revenue

**Calculates Net Profit:**
```
Revenue - COGS = Gross Profit
Gross Profit - Expenses = Net Profit
```

---

### 4. PROFIT ANALYSIS

**Auto-Generates P&L:**

```
OPERATING EXPENSES         $49,166
NET PROFIT                 $98,332 (40%)
```

**Also Includes:**
